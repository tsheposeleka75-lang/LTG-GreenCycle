Our Vision

🤝

Entrepreneur Model

Entrepreneurs receive training, GIS data, compliance support, and brand credibility while contributing to local implementation.

How the Programme Works

A simple three-step community-driven model designed for sustainable impact.

1

Community Participation

Community members join local Green Cycle cooperatives focusing on recycling, waste management, and environmental services. These cooperatives form the foundation of community ownership.

2

Training & Skills

Participants receive structured training through SETA-accredited partners, focusing on recycling practices, environmental management, cooperative operations, and green entrepreneurship.

3

Implementation

Trained cooperatives and entrepreneurs participate in pilot projects including waste collection, waste-to-product manufacturing, and community recycling initiatives.
